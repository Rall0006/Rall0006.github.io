About Kai

Hello, my name is Kai Rallison. I am interested in many different subjects such as experimenting with ethnic foods, listening to KPop music, watching animee, and historical movies and reading a wide variety of material including Manga, Fairytales, Animal Care, and Adventure to name a few. Growing up in the country, I am fond of animals and enjoy working with them. All of this feeds into my creativity and allows me to create a variety of images. I have attended Durham College for Game Development and bring this experience to my current studies in Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College. I am passionate about creating images and media content that bring a subject to life and engage the viewer.
